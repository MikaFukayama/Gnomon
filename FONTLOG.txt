FONTLOG for Umbra*
===================

This file includes information about the Umbra* typeface by indestructible type*.


About
-----

Umbra* is an original OpenType variable font created by indestructible type*. It is inspired by
World War Two posters, and is made to display the capabilities of the OpenType variable font
system.

Umbra* is designed and maintained by Owen Earl (https://plus.google.com/103790404905174393616), 
who is the creator of the font foundry indestructible type* (http://indestructible-type.github.io).
